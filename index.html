About

Research at the intersection of spatial data, AI, and lived urban experience.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Geospatial and Environmental Analysis at Virginia Tech. My dissertation work focuses on enhancing urban spaces with GeoAI and visual language models.

I also pursue an M.Eng. in Computer Science and Applications, bringing together geospatial analysis, machine learning, urban computing, and human-centered sensing.
